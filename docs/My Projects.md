## Mods

### VoluBind

VoluBind is a client-sided Fabric mod for 1.19 that allows you to create keybinds to control your in-game volume.

* [GitHub](https://github.com/jadelily18/VoluBind)
* [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/volubind)
